Feed The Beast

 

Feed The Beast is a group of people that specialise in making high quality modpacks and maps for Minecraft. We started out as a skyblock style challenge map that focused on the use of tech style mods. These maps became extremely popular and in order to allow more people access to the maps the FTB Launcher was created.

 

The team at FTB have worked closely with CreeperHost since almost day 1 in order to help ensure our players get the best possible experience from online server play. We are also working with Curse to help design and create a new client for Minecraft which will bring more awesome features to the community.

Mod Packs

Feed The Beast offers many different styles of modpacks to the community, catering to all tastes. From FTB’s Monster and the Direwolf20 pack catering to all round play to more focused packs like Tech and Magic world.  We also host some of the best 3rd party packs ever made including Agrarian Skies, BloodNBones, Crash Landing and many more.  You can find more details on some of our packs here or on the launcher.

 

Maps & Texture Packs

The launcher also gives you the ability to install many different texture packs and maps specific to the pack you are playing. They can be installed at the click of a button and include maps played by the youtubers that play the packs, challenge maps to test your knowledge of the mods and some of your favorite texture packs with one click installs. You can download and install texutre packs or modpacks via launcher.
